# ReqMaster AI - Presentation Script
*Duration: 5-7 minutes*

---

## 1. INTRODUCTION (30 seconds)
"Hello everyone! Today I'm presenting **ReqMaster AI** - an intelligent requirements gathering platform that revolutionizes how we collect and document software requirements.

Traditional requirement gathering is time-consuming, error-prone, and often results in incomplete documentation. ReqMaster AI solves this using conversational AI to extract, categorize, and document requirements automatically."

---

## 2. THE PROBLEM (30 seconds)
"Let me show you the challenges we're addressing:
- Manual requirement gathering takes hours of meetings and documentation
- Important details often get missed or misunderstood
- Requirements are scattered across emails, notes, and documents
- No standardized format for different stakeholders
- Difficult to track progress during requirement sessions"

---

## 3. LIVE DEMO - PART 1: Getting Started (1 minute)
**[Show Login Screen]**
"The application starts with a clean authentication system. Users can:
- Create an account for persistent project storage
- Login to access previous projects
- Or continue as a guest for quick sessions"

**[Login and show Dashboard]**
"Once logged in, you see your dashboard with:
- All recent projects at a glance
- Quick access to start new sessions
- Project management with delete functionality"

---

## 4. LIVE DEMO - PART 2: Intelligent Conversation (2 minutes)
**[Click 'Start Interview']**
"Now, let's gather requirements for a project. Watch the **Progress Tracker** at the top."

**[Type: "I want to build a task management app for teams"]**

"Notice several AI-powered features:
- **Progress tracking** showing conversation stages
- **Voice input** for hands-free interaction
- **Text-to-Speech** toggle to hear AI responses
- **File upload** for sketches or documents

The AI intelligently:
- Asks focused, relevant questions
- Extracts functional, non-functional, and domain requirements
- Identifies potential risks automatically
- Suggests timelines"

**[Have brief conversation - 2-3 exchanges]**

## 5. LIVE DEMO - PART 3: Smart Features (1 minute)
**[Show during conversation]**
"Let me demonstrate our unique features:

1. **Voice Input** - Click the microphone to speak your requirements
2. **File Upload** - Attach wireframes, sketches, or documents
3. **Text-to-Speech** - Toggle to hear AI responses read aloud
4. **Progress Tracking** - Visual stages from Introduction to Finalization"

## 6. LIVE DEMO - PART 4: Requirements Analysis (2 minutes)
**[Click 'Analyze & Finish']**
"When ready, the AI analyzes the entire conversation and generates structured documentation."

**[Show Summary View]**
"Look at the comprehensive output:
- **Categorized Requirements**: Functional, Non-Functional, and Domain-specific
- **Priority Levels**: High, Medium, Low
- **Risk Analysis**: Automatically identified concerns
- **Timeline Estimation**: Based on complexity
- **Edit Functionality**: Modify, delete, or add requirements"

**[Show Export Options]**
"Multiple export formats:
- **SRS Template** - IEEE-style formal documentation

- **Simple TXT** - Quick plain text export
- **PDF** - Print-ready professional document"

## DEMO TIPS:
1. **Before presenting:** Create a sample project to show
2. **Have a backup:** Screenshot key screens in case of technical issues
3. **Practice timing:** Aim for 5-7 minutes total
4. **Be interactive:** Invite questions during the demo
5. **Show real use case:** Use a relatable project example
6. **Highlight uniqueness:** Emphasize AI + multi-modal features
